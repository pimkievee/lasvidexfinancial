## Recommended Content Structure

Each new page or article should include:

- One clear H1 using the target keyword naturally.
- 3-5 H2 sections answering common search questions.
- A short FAQ section where appropriate.
- Internal links to `services.html`, `contact.html`, and related topic pages.
- A clear CTA using either `Request Financial Guidance` or `Book Consultation`.
- Page-specific title, meta description, canonical, Open Graph, Twitter card, and JSON-LD schema.
